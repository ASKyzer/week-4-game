Rules

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four Pokemon characters below. By clicking on a character, you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to the random number. You lose if your score goes above that number.

The value of each monster is hidden from you until you click on it.

Each time when the game starts, the game will change the value of each monster.

Enjoy the game and go catch 'em all!
